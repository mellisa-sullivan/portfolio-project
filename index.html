Shaping the customer experience from the code-up.

Hi there, and welcome to my portfolio. I'm Mellisa Sullivan, a software developer based out of Boston, Massachusetts.

I have a strong background in roles that have allowed me to shape the customer experience face-to-face, which has granted me the ability to provide a consumer-oriented perspective to all projects I tackle.

About Me

Having begun my career in strictly customer-facing roles, I have seen first-hand what creates a positive (and negative) customer experience, and how that can impact the growth and perception of a brand as a whole. The best compliment I can get from a customer is, "You made that so easy for me."

Being part of a team that loves what they do, and does it well, is what gets me out of bed in the morning. I love being able to go to work, and learn from the people around me. I currently specialize in HTML, CSS, and Javascript websites and applications.

When I'm not coding, you can most likely find me in the kitchen, whipping up whatever recipe I've set my mind to that day. Not in the kitchen? Maybe I'm making tie dyes for my other venture, Northbound Dyes! If I'm not there... well, just find my dog, and you'll find me.
